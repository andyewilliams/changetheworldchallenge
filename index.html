Welcome to the Change the World University Challenge

The Change the World University Challenge is an innovative and transformative contest designed to engage university students in addressing some of the most pressing global challenges of our time. The contest encourages participants to explore groundbreaking concepts such as collective intelligence, the technology gravity well, and the collective social brain hypothesis.

Through this challenge, students will have the opportunity to propose creative solutions that harness the power of collective intelligence to tackle issues like economic inequality, climate change, and technological disruption. Our Mission

The Change the World University Challenge seeks to ignite innovative thinking and inspire action by engaging students and academic communities to explore cutting-edge ideas such as collective intelligence, the technology gravity well, and the collective social brain hypothesis. By fostering intellectual discourse and collaboration, we aim to address pressing global challenges like economic inequality, climate change, and technological displacement. Through smarter cooperation, we can create solutions that elevate societies and ensure a prosperous future for all.
